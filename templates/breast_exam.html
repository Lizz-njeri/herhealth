How to Perform a Breast Self-Examination

Step 1: Visual Inspection

Stand in front of a mirror and look for any visible changes such as lumps, swelling, or skin changes. Raise your arms above your head and inspect again for any changes.

Step 2: Manual Inspection

Use the pads of your fingers to examine your breasts in a circular motion. Start from the outermost part of the breast and work your way in. Do this while standing up and lying down.

Step 3: Check for Abnormalities

Pay attention to any lumps, unusual pain, skin changes, or nipple discharge. If you find anything unusual, consult with a healthcare professional immediately.
